# Common learner errors — Italian

- **essere/avere in passato prossimo.** Motion/change/reflexives take essere
  with agreement (siamo andatE); transitives take avere. When wrong, ask:
  does the verb have an object?
- **Article selection.** lo/gli before s+consonant, z, gn, ps (lo studente,
  gli zii). Drill the article WITH each new noun.
- **Preposition+article contractions.** in + il = nel, su + la = sulla, di +
  i = dei. Parse them aloud until automatic.
- **Subjunctive after opinion.** "Penso che è" → sia. Teach penso che/credo
  che/è importante che as subjunctive triggers.
- **ci/ne omission.** "Hai libri?" "Sì, ho tre" → ne ho tre. Spanish/English
  speakers drop these; drill answers that require them.
- **Double consonant misses.** pala/palla, caro/carro are different words —
  coach spelling and pronunciation together.
- **Imperfetto vs passato prossimo.** Same coaching as Spanish: backbone vs
  background, two-clause stories.
