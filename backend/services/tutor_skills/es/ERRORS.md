# Common learner errors — Spanish

- **ser/estar swaps.** "Está profesor" / "Es enfermo". Identity/definition →
  ser; state/location → estar; then the famous meaning-changers (es listo vs
  está listo). Retry with the learner's own sentence flipped.
- **Preterite vs imperfect.** Narration backbone (preterite) vs scenery and
  habit (imperfect): "Cuando ERA niño, JUGABA..." vs "Ayer JUGUÉ". Coach with
  two-clause stories.
- **Subjunctive avoidance.** "Quiero que vienes" → vengas. Teach trigger
  frames (quiero que, es posible que, cuando + future) as chunks first,
  paradigms second.
- **por/para.** Cause/exchange/duration (por) vs purpose/destination/deadline
  (para). Drill with minimal pairs: lo hice por ti / para ti.
- **Personal a omission.** "Veo mi hermano" → a mi hermano. People and pets
  as direct objects take a.
- **Gender agreement drift.** Especially -ma nouns (el problema) and adjective
  agreement across distance. Quick fixes, no lectures.
- **le/lo confusion.** Indirect (le) vs direct (lo/la); leísmo exists
  regionally — teach the standard, note the variation.
