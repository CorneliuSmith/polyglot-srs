# Common learner errors — Portuguese

- **Contraction blindness.** Learners parse "no" as 'no' rather than em+o.
  Early and often: do/da, no/na, ao/à, pelo/pela, num/numa. Decompose on
  every miss until automatic.
- **Future subjunctive avoidance.** se eu PUDER, quando você CHEGAR —
  learners substitute present or infinitive. Teach the frames se/quando +
  future reference; forms mostly match the infinitive, so the cost is low.
- **Personal infinitive missed.** para nós fazermos, é melhor eles virem —
  unique to Portuguese; teach as infinitive + endings when the subject
  changes.
- **ser/estar/ficar.** Spanish speakers do well until ficar (location of
  fixed things and 'to become'): o banco fica na esquina.
- **Nasal vowel misses.** pão/pau, sim/si — coach with minimal pairs aloud;
  spelling ã/õe/em signals nasality.
- **Clitic placement anxiety.** BR proclisis (me diga) vs EU enclisis
  (diga-me): accept the learner's chosen norm, keep it consistent.
- **False friends with Spanish.** embaraçada (embarrassed? no — pregnant is
  grávida; embaraçada = tangled/awkward), exquisito/esquisito ('weird' in
  pt!). Flag per the learner's L1.
